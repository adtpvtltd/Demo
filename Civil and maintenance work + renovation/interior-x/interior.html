About us

Cathie Hong Interiors is a boutique interior design firm based in the South Bay Area. Our team of designers specializes in blending our modern aesthetic with each client’s personal design style. We take on a wide range of projects, including new construction projects, full-home renovations, and furnishing and styling projects.

Services

FULL SERVICE DESIGN

  • a detailed design questionnaire that helps us to understand the scope and needs of the project, as well as your specific design style
  • design plan including preliminary sketches, CAD drawings and elevations, and 3D renderings if needed
  • close collaboration with architects, contractors, and other tradespeople
  • comprehensive material & finish selections for renovations
  • furniture, lighting, accessory selections for furnishings
  • detailed specification sheets to track all product costs and lead times
  • project management and regular site visits to monitor design execution
  • purchasing and procurement
  • installation and final touches

Our preference as designers is to be brought on to the project as early in the process as possible.
