Emergency Exit Seats

Seats in row 12 and 14 are Emergency Exit seats and can be booked as part of Vistara Select. The assignment of these select seats are complimentary for our Club Vistara Platinum members. Please note, an Emergency Exit seat cannot be allocated to children, senior citizens or anyone with any medical condition or unable to assist during an emergency.

Please note that the Emergency Exit Row Seat numbers specified here might differ depending on the aircraft configuration. Please refer to the live Seat Map while selecting your seats.

Customers can choose the seat of their choice under Vistara Select. Click here to view the seat fee.
